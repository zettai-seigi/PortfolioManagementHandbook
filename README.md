# IT Portfolio Management Handbook

A comprehensive guide to IT Portfolio Management best practices, aligned with ITIL and industry standards.

## Overview

This handbook provides practical guidance for implementing and optimizing IT Portfolio Management processes. It covers investment prioritization, portfolio balancing, governance structures, and benefits realization.

**Live Site:** https://zettai-seigi.github.io/PortfolioManagementHandbook/

## Key Frameworks

### Investment Categories
- **Transform** - New capabilities, innovation (15-25%)
- **Grow** - Expand existing capabilities (20-30%)
- **Run** - Maintain operations (40-50%)
- **Comply/Secure** - Regulatory/security (10-15%)

### Critical Success Factors
1. Executive Sponsorship and Governance
2. Disciplined Investment Process
3. Transparent Prioritization Criteria
4. Portfolio Management Office (PMO)
5. Benefits Accountability
6. Regular Portfolio Reviews
7. Integrated Tools
8. Continuous Improvement

## Applicable Standards

- ITIL 4 - Service Portfolio Management
- PMI Standard for Portfolio Management
- SAFe Lean Portfolio Management
- Val IT Framework
- COBIT 2019
